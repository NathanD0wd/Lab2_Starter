This Weeks Best Runs

The highlight of this week was Nathan's first time beating gold stake, which is also the first time this has been done in club history. The strategy of choice was to use blue deck. Not only did Nathan beat gold stake on blue deck, but also on spectral deck, where he was able to find a seed with a polychrome eternal brainstorm, as well as several other spectacular jokers to make a gold facecard build.

Other notable news this weeks is the addition of our first new member, Santiago Reyes. He was convinced to join after conversation with Nathan regarding the YouTuber Skoottie. After a night of playing Balatro on Nathan's computer, Santi decided to buy the game for himself, a fantastic financial decision. We love supporting a solo developer.

Joker of the Week

The Joker of the Week is Wee Joker. Wee Joker is a very unique joker, not only because it's the only one that has a separate sizing, but it is also very unique in that it can get very high base chips, which can result in a very challenging run.
